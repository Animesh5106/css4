A shoe is a type of footwear. It is an item of clothing. Shoes come in pairs, with one shoe for each foot.There are many different types of shoes. Athletic shoes, for example, are to make running, walking or jogging easier by making the weight of the shoe lighter and the sole of the shoe softer. Leather shoes are formal shoes. They are worn for business and ceremonial functions.
